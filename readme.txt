This is a classic 2d top down game where you control a car and kill all the enemies that chase you and get highest score.

Enemy drops:
Energy refill
repair kit
power up

Element counter list:
2x damage and 2x score
Fire>Nature
Nature>Water
Water>Fire
Dark<>Light

1.0x damage
White<>Every element

Player Movement:
Move: WAD

Fire: left click
cost one energy

EMP: R
Discard all the projectiles(include yours) on screen
cost 100 energy

Switich guns:
1:single shot
2:single shot back and front
3:shortgun
4:shortgun back and front
5:360' no scope

Switich elements: 
Q:Fire 
E:Nature 
Left Shift:Water 
Space:Dark or Light

Exit:Escape
